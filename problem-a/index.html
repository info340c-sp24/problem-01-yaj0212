Dorothy Gale

Dorothy Gale is the main character from a children's novel The Wonder Wizard of Oz, written by American author L. Frank Baum, first published in 1900. The novel tells the story of a young girl named Dorothy and her dog, who are swept away by a tornado to the fantastical land of Oz. There, she encounters a group of friends and embarks on a journey to find her way back home and fulfill their individual desires. Dorothy Gale is a fascinating character for several reasons, not least because of her embodiment of innocence, bravery, and a relentless pursuit of truth and justice.
